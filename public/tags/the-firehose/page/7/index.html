Wordpress woes.

2011-01-06

I’m extremely irritated by the one last blog that I host on wordpress that I just can’t seem to get converted to any other format. Wordpress, while a very nice publishing platform, isn’t quite compliant with the XML standard in the way that they format their export files. So, what that means for anyone that has a decent sized blog that they want to move to anything other than wordpress, is that they may be out of luck.
