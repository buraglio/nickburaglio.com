More new shirts from Found Item Clothing!

2008-12-04
Share
  Tweet  

As many know, I love T-Shirts, and the only thing better than a great T-Shirt, is a great shirt from a classic 80’s movie of my childhood.
Thank goodness there is Found Item Clothing. These guys recreate so many of the shirts from classic pop culture and just all around great movies (they also have a pretty good movie/dvd review section).
Anyway, in keeping with their awesome tradition, they’ve released even more new shirts. I mean, come on, where else are you going to find the shirt that Jack Burton wore in Big Trouble in Little China or the Skeleton shirt from This is Spinal Tap pains takingly recreated in every detail?

Hell, I got the Bunny Slippers from Real Genius for my wife last year for xmas.

Well, my friends, they’ve done it again.
